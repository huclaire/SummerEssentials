Bucket List


Now that school’s out, we finally have time to do everything we’ve been craving since September! All the little things such as eating watermelon by the pool and chasing ice cream trucks, to all the big things like going on road trips and getting out of the country.

This bucket list will help you organize your ideas, provide inspiration, and be a guideline for your fun summer days.

Let’s get this summer started!







Summer Bucket List 2019

  • Get nails done but make them really fancy or unique.
  • Go thrift shopping and turn old clothes into trendy new ones.
  • Help people at a disadvantage. E.g homeless people, people with disabilities.
  • Go on a day trip with friends out of the city.
  • Have/go to a pool party.
  • Have a beach picnic
  • Rent bikes downtown and discover new areas of the city.
  • Pull an all nighter
  • Get a summer job
  • Spend 5 days without a phone
  • Listen to a new music genre
